Run the devupdater in an empty folder to install Infinity Loader (remove from the Paradise Bundle folder)

Make sure the folder is whitelisted from your antivirus!

Setup IL

On your pc, go to Documents 

Make a folder called "InfinityLoader"

Open it, and then make another one called "Projects"

Open it, and then make another one called "Bundle"

Open it, and extract the Paradise Bundle folder here (after removing the InfinityLoader - Dev Build.zip)

Start IL and click on Compiler, open project

Click on the blue box and you should see Paradise Bundle. Double click it

Open the config.il to select your game, mode and platform

Click compile and voila

Enjoy!
